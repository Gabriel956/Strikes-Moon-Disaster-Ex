1st Preliminary Delivery

This project will be a continuation of our 3D game, "Strikes Moon Disaster". 

The genre 

The main gameplay will consist of 3 levels, which will have different objectives. One will require you to kidnap astronauts under a time limit.

As a ufo you will be able to pick up astronauts with a tractor beam, shoot them from above, and navigate in all directions. 

You win once you complete the main objective of each level. You lose if you take too much damage from various sources.

We plan to keep track of your score, current health, and timer either increasing or decreasing.
