# Moon Discovery Strikes

## Core Idea

You're an alien UFO pilot exploring a moon doing important *moon research* when suddenly... *MOON DISCOVERY STRIKES!*

You've just discovered a new species of bipedal hairless apes wearing funny puffy moon suits. Now it's your job to ~~abduct~~ collect a few for *moon research purposes* to learn more about these silly little moon apes and report back to the your home world. Maybe they're friendly!

## Details

### Mechanics

In this 3D sci-fi survival/collection game, you pilot a UFO that flies around some lunar terrain and can abduct and fire projectiles below it. It consists of three levels.

### UI Plan

The UI will display your health in the upper left corner and the level's remaining time in the upper right. The third level is an exception to this as it is high score based. Instead of the level's remaining time, it will display your current score.

### Level 1

You just discovered human astronauts on the moon, but oh no! There's a meteor shower! You must collect enough specimens before time runs while avoiding taking damage to your ship!

#### Win Condition

Abduct enough astronauts before time runs out.

#### Lose Condition

Run out of time or take too much damage from meteors.

### Level 2

Your home world is pleased with your discovery and tasks you with collecting more samples. The meteor show hasn't let up, but worse than that, it seems these moon apes have constructed a moon shelter with some kind of primitive projectile weapon. And they're aiming it at you! You must collect the samples you've been tasked to before either time runs out or you get shot out of the moon sky!

#### Win Condition

Abduct enough astronauts before time runs out.

#### Lose Condition

Run out of time or take too much damage from meteors or projectiles.

### Level 3

After your last moon encounter, your home world has determined these moon apes are more moon than they can handle. For the safety of the galaxy or something, you have to eliminate as many of these dangerous moon fiends as you can. You're UFO has now been equipped with a weapon to fight back against the moon mob. The meteor shower has picked up, and so has their barrage. Good moon luck.

#### Win Condition

Survive as long as possible and kill as many astronauts as you can before you die.

#### Lose Condition

Not applicable. This level is high score based.
